FAQ

Quick answers.

How does the 3-day trial work?
Sign in with Google and you're in. The trial gives you every feature for 72 hours. No card. When the trial ends you're sent to the subscribe page — choose a plan and pay via UPI/bank transfer to continue.
How do I pay?
Choose a plan, contact us via the email on the subscribe page, and we'll share UPI / bank details. Once payment is received, your subscription is activated within a few hours — usually under 30 minutes during market hours.
What happens if I renew before my current subscription ends?
New days stack on top of your existing expiry. You never lose unused days. Renew on day 25 of a 30-day plan and you get 35 days of access total.
Can I use one account on multiple devices?
No. Each account is bound to one device using browser fingerprinting. If you change devices (new laptop, lost phone), contact support to approve the new device — old device is auto-revoked.
Is this financial advice?
No. We provide technical analysis tools — scores, signals, filters, charts. Nothing on the platform constitutes investment advice. We are not SEBI-registered advisors. Always do your own research and consult a qualified financial advisor before investing.
Can I get a refund?
Use the 3-day free trial to evaluate the product before paying. Once a subscription is active, we don't offer refunds — but you can stop renewing anytime.
